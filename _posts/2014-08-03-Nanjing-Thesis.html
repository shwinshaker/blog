Nanjing PM2.5 air pollution investigation and manage methods
Abstract
With the rapid economic developments and the advance of urbanization and
industrialization, air pollution has been becoming more and more serious in China .At the same
time, PM2.5 was introduced to the public as a novel notion by the advance of the detection
technology and standard improvement. Because the
D
"
of PM2.5 is under 2.5

introduces the chemical component, source, harmfulness, toxic mechanism and preventive
treatment of PM2.5. Meanwhile, based on the data of Nanjing air quality, a report to Nanjing
PM2.5 condition will be displayed.
